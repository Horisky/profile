01 · Overview01 · 概述 Why为什么

A print-on-demand design factory. Feed it a list of themes — bands, characters, memes — and it scrapes references, generates T-shirt prints in batches, screens every design against a trademark / infringement word list, and exports ready-to-list product URLs.

一套印花量产工厂。喂给它一份题材清单 —— 乐队、角色、网络梗 —— 它会抓取参考图、批量生成 T 恤印花、对每张设计做商标 / 侵权词筛查，再导出可直接上架的商品 URL。

One operator turns a theme list into hundreds of sellable variations. Built-in infringement-word screening and a review queue keep the output store-safe; the whole loop — scrape, generate, test, screen, ship — lives in one workspace.

一个人就能把一份题材清单变成上百款可售变体。内置侵权词筛查和审核队列保证产出合规；抓图、生成、测试、筛查、上架的整条链路都在一个工作台里。
